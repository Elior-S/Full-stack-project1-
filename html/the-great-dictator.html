The Great Dictator (1940)

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Director: Charlie Chaplin

Cast: Charlie Chaplin, Paulette Goddard, Jack Oakie

Synopsis : Calvin Joyner, once voted “Most Likely to Succeed” in high school, is now an accountant leading a quiet and predictable life. Everything changes when he reconnects with Bob Stone, a former classmate who used to be an overweight, bullied kid but has since become a lethal CIA agent. Dragged into a world of espionage, Calvin is forced to help Bob with a top-secret mission involving stolen satellite codes and dangerous enemies. As the situation spirals out of control, the unlikely duo must work together to survive, uncover the truth, and maybe even save the world — all while delivering plenty of chaos and laughs.
