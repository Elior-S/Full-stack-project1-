Genre: Fantasy, Action

Director: Chris Columbus

Genre: Action, Comedy

Director: Rawson Marshall Thurber

Cast: Dwayne Johnson, Kevin Hart, Amy Ryan, Aaron Paul

Synopsis : Calvin Joyner, once voted “Most Likely to Succeed” in high school, is now an accountant leading a quiet and predictable life. The Great Dictator (1940)

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Director: Charlie Chaplin

Cast: Charlie Chaplin, Paulette Goddard, Jack Oakie

Synopsis: In a fictional country on the brink of war, a humble Jewish barber unknowingly shares an identical appearance with the ruthless dictator Adenoid Hynkel. Through a series of coincidences and misunderstandings, their identities begin to blur, leading to a dramatic switch. Don't Mess with the Zohan (2008)

Genre: Action, Comedy

Director: Dennis Dugan

Cast: Adam Sandler, John Turturro, Emmanuelle Chriqui, Nick Swardson
