Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (2001)

Genre: Fantasy, Action

Director: Chris Columbus

Cast: Daniel Radcliffe, Emma Watson, Rupert Grint

Synopsis :Harry Potter, an orphan raised by his neglectful aunt and uncle, discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he befriends Ron and Hermione and enters a magical world filled with wonders and mysteries. As Harry adjusts to his new life, he learns about the existence of a powerful object — the Philosopher’s Stone — sought after by dark forces. Throughout the school year, Harry and his friends uncover clues, face dangerous challenges, and ultimately confront a threat linked to the dark wizard Voldemort. Determined and courageous, Harry begins to understand his past, his destiny, and the true meaning of friendship and bravery.
